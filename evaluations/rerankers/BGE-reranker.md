Answer: To apply for graduation, students must submit the "Apply for Graduation" form via the Duke Kunshan University student information system. Students are responsible for submitting the form by established deadlines, and information about the online diploma form's availability is sent to their Duke Kunshan University email. Question: What are the steps to change my major?
Answer: To change your major, follow these steps:  
1. **Check eligibility**: Ensure the major you wish to pursue was available in the Academic Bulletin of your matriculation year or subsequent years.  
2. **Contact the Office of Undergraduate Advising**: If the major’s requirements have been updated, reach out to them (refer to the section on Major Requirements in Part 3) to meet the revised requirements.  
3. **Complete DKU 101**: Satisfactorily finish the non-credit DKU 101 course as specified in Part 3 of the curriculum.  

For detailed guidelines, consult the Academic Bulletin and the Office of Undergraduate Advising.

Question: What happens if I am late on my tuition payment?
Answer: If you are late on your tuition payment, you will not be allowed to register for future terms and may be administratively withdrawn from the University. Access to academic transcripts, credit certification, and diploma issuance will be restricted. If the account remains outstanding after you leave the University, it may be referred to a collection agency and reported to a credit bureau.
