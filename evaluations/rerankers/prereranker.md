Question: What are the steps to change my major?
Answer: To change your major, follow these steps:  
1. **Check eligibility**: Ensure the major you wish to pursue was available in the Academic Bulletin of the year you matriculated or is an updated version of an existing major.  
2. **Contact the Office of Undergraduate Advising**: Reach out to them to discuss meeting the updated requirements for the major (as outlined in the "Major Requirements" section of Part 3).  
3. **Complete DKU 101 (if required)**: Satisfactorily complete the non-credit DKU 101 course, as noted in section 2.  

Note: You cannot select majors published only in years subsequent to your matriculation year unless explicitly allowed through updated requirements.

Question: What happens if I am late on my tuition payment?

Past due accounts restrict access to academic transcripts, credit certification, and diploma issuance. Outstanding balances after leaving the university may be referred to a collection agency and reported to a credit bureau.
